Why Watuh

We Build Bottles That Last a Lifetime.

Every Watuh bottle is crafted from professional-grade 18/8 stainless steel and sealed with our proprietary TempShield™ double-wall vacuum insulation technology. No plastic liners, no compromises.

We believe that great hydration shouldn't cost the planet. Our manufacturing process uses 30% recycled materials, and we plant one tree for every bottle sold through our Roots Program.
